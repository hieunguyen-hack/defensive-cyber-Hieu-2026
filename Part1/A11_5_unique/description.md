2. Iris Recognition Scanner:

An iris recognition scanner is a highly accurate, contactless biometric technology that uses infrared light to capture high-contrast images of the intricate, unique patterns in the colored ring of the eye

3. Mobile Access Readers:

Mobile Access Readers are devices used in access control systems that let people use their smartphones instead of physical cards or keys to unlock doors.
Users gain entry to buildings by approaching the Bluetooth enabled readers at the access points and unlock the door with their smart phones eliminating the need for physical keys or access cards.

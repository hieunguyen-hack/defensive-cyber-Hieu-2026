VPNs (Virtual Private Networks):

A virtual private network, or VPN, is an encrypted connection that secures data transmission between devices over the Internet.

Personal VPNs: They encrypt internet traffic, mask IP addresses, and protect internet users on public Wi-Fi.

Business VPNs: They offer centralized management, role-based access control, and dedicated infrastructure.

Function: A VPN encrypts internet traffic and routes it through a remote server, hiding the user’s IP address and securing data from unauthorized access.

There are many different use cases for VPNs personal or business
